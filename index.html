About us

Society for industrial management and engineering provides a platform for the BIT Mesra undergraduates through industrial visits, event seminar, webinars, research projects, workshops, annual meets from time to time.

It is a rich platform where industry based science and engineering is given importance and opportunity for students to come forward to enhance their skill which will eventually help them after graduating from the college.
